YWAM Amsterdam is part of the worldwide missions movement Youth With a Mission which exists to know God and to make Him known.

Innovative

With over 40 years of innovation in ministry, YWAM Amsterdam continues to be a birthplace for new initiatives to reach the world with Jesus’ love.

Urban

Our ministry happens right out of the front door due to YWAM Amsterdam’s unique locations in the heart of this vibrant European city.

Community driven

We seek to have a relationship oriented approach, passionately following Jesus as a multicultural and diverse family.
